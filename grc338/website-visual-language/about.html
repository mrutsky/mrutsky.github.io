SLO Brew Coffee House has been making happy coffee since January of 2014. The combination of scratch made pastries, craft roasted coffee, and curated goods in a creative space makes stepping into Scout a memorable and magical experience.

Welcome to our cozy and stylish coffee house, where we are proud to serve the finest quality coffee from around the world. Our passion for coffee is contagious, and we aim to provide our customers with an exceptional experience that is second to none. Whether you are a coffee connoisseur or someone who simply enjoys a good cup of Joe, we have something for everyone.

At our coffee house, we take pride in sourcing our beans from ethical and sustainable sources to ensure that our coffee is not only great tasting but also environmentally responsible. We have a range of single-origin coffees, specialty blends, and delectable flavors that are carefully selected to suit the diverse tastes of our customers. We work closely with our suppliers to ensure that we get the best quality beans that are roasted to perfection to bring out the unique flavor profiles.

Our coffee house is not just a place to grab a quick cup of coffee; it's a community where people can come together to relax, meet friends, work, or just unwind. Our friendly and knowledgeable baristas are trained to offer personalized service and are always happy to chat about coffee or recommend your next favorite brew. We also have a range of snacks and pastries that pair perfectly with our coffee, making it the ideal spot for breakfast, lunch or a snack break. Come and join us for a cup of coffee today, and you'll find out why we are one of the best coffee houses in town!
